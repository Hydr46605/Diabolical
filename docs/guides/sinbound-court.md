# Sinbound Court

The Sinbound Court is Diabolical's high-sin pressure system. Debt already calls
collectors; sin now calls court mobs.

## Court Mobs

| Mob | Trigger |
| --- | --- |
| Contract Imp | natural pressure from sin 25, or failed Red Moon backlash |
| Sin Wraith | natural pressure from sin 75, or Sinners Effigy |
| Ledger Bailiff | mixed debt 35 and sin 25, or Bailiff Bell |
| Ashen Advocate | Court Candle or severe audit pressure |
| The Accuser | sin 150 or Black Writ |

Only one Sinbound Court encounter runs at a time. Defeating court mobs can pay
small amounts of debt or sin. The Accuser is the capstone case and can leave an
Accusers Seal for stronger recovery.

## Court Items

Crafted court items are used from the Infernal Ledger's Court page while held:

| Item | Use |
| --- | --- |
| Black Writ | calls The Accuser and adds sin |
| Court Candle | calls an Ashen Advocate |
| Bailiff Bell | calls a Ledger Bailiff |
| Sinners Effigy | baits a Sin Wraith |
| Ashen Brief | cleanses sin without starting a fight |
| Accusers Seal | rare recovery item from The Accuser |

Operators can grant the craftable items with:

```mcfunction
function diabolical:debug/give_court_items
```

Debug starts are also available under `diabolical:mobs/debug/`.
